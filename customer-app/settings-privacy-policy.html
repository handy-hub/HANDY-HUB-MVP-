Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide (name, email, phone, location), usage data (searches, bookings, messages), and device information (OS, app version, device ID).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to match you with artisans, process payments, personalise your experience, send service notifications, and improve our platform. We do not sell your personal data.

3. Data Sharing

We share limited data with artisans you book (name, phone, address for the job). We use third-party services like Firebase for authentication and data storage, which have their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored on Google Firebase servers. We use encryption and access controls to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your personal information at any time via the app. To request deletion of all your data, use the Delete Account option in Settings or email privacy@handyhub.com.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to remember your preferences (language, currency, theme). No advertising cookies are used.

7. Children's Privacy

Handy Hub is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the app.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@handyhub.com.
